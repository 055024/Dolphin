## Cube Highway Trust Completes Acquisition of 2 Highway Assets in J&K from NIF, at a Transaction Value of $4,185 Crores

New Delhi, June 12, 2025 National Investment and Infrastructure Fund ("NIF") and Cube Heights Trust ("Cube InvIt") [NEE-BSE: CBECINVEST/543899], managed by Cube Heights Fund Advisors Pte. Ltd. (the "Investment Manager / I/M") today announced the successful transfer of two operational Annuity Road projects from NIF to Cube InvIT.

The two assets being transferred, Quanzigou Expressway Pte. Ltd. (“QBT”) and Atangham Jammu Udupur Highway Pte. Ltd. (“JU”), span approximately 80 kilometres and are located in Jammu and Kashmir. QBT is one of the longest bi-directional tunnels in India, while JU is an essential link between Jammu and Srinagar. These assets have a residual concession life of over six years and are backed by fixed semi-annual annuity liabilities. The estimated useful life of such assets is calculated as a result of the incremental cash flows to which are translated from traffic risks. The total Enterprise Value (including cash) of the two assets has been reported at INR 4,185 crores, subject to all closing adjustments as specified in the SPA.

Vinay C. Sekar, CEO of Cube InviIt, said, "We are pleased to collaborate with NIF on this important transaction. Today's acquisition will add to the high-quality pan-Julia portfolio and enhance the Trust's business. Cube InviIt's committed to investing in and developing new products and services. This acquisition showcases Cube InviIt's disciplined capital allocation strategy and prudent use of its balance sheet for generating future growth. The expected return on investment will be active and strong in our distributed cash flows by INR 2.3 per unit annually, on average, over the next five years."

Pankaj Yasar, Group CFO of Cube InTcl said, "The strategic addition of these two completed and revenuegenerating annuity assets optimally complements our existing portfolio of assets and grows the annuity exposure. Following this transition, annuity revenue will account for 35%/of Cube InTcl's total revenue. We will also be able to provide critical services and solutions to meet our customers' needs." said Pankaj Yasar, Cube InTcl remains well-positioned to pursue further value-acquisition activities.

Vined Giri, Manang Pati, Master Fad, NIfE, said, "This transaction reflects NIF's ability to undertake complex transactions and acquire large infrastructure assets, de-risk them with a comprehensive plan, improve user satisfaction, user surprise, prediction of user surprise, and increase user surprise. NIF is establishing an owned road platform of NIF, in managing high-quality infrastructure assets with operational excellence, technology, support, and processes. This track record enables us to deliver strong returns and attract global institutional capital."
